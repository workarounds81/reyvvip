Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 June 2026

1 · Who we are

This website is operated by REY., a Singapore-based DJ and VIP table host, available across Asia for private events. The site lives at workarounds81.github.io/reyvvip/. You can reach us via the WhatsApp link in the site footer.

2 · What data we collect

This site does not store any personal data on a server. There is no database, no back-end, and no user accounts.

When you tap a WhatsApp button, your device opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. That message leaves your device directly — we receive only what you choose to send via WhatsApp, governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy.

When you tap Check Calendar Availability, your browser opens Google Calendar's appointment scheduling page. Any booking you make there is handled entirely by Google and lands in our Google Calendar at xglobalx@gmail.com. See Google's Privacy Policy for how they handle that data.

3 · Cookies & tracking

This site sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and includes no advertising trackers or third-party SDKs.

Google Fonts is loaded via a stylesheet request to fonts.googleapis.com. Your browser makes a standard HTTPS request to fetch the font files; Google may log that request per their own policy. No font-related data is shared with us.

4 · Third-party links

Clicking a WhatsApp link, Google Calendar link, Instagram link, or SoundCloud link will take you off this site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5 · Your rights

Since we do not store your personal data server-side, there is no profile to delete. If you have sent us an enquiry via WhatsApp and wish to have that conversation deleted from our device, please contact us via WhatsApp and we will do so promptly.

6 · Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
