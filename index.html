04FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything on VIP table access, pricing, clubs and private bookings in Singapore.

How does VIP table access work in Singapore?
You buy individual slots at a premium host table instead of paying a full bottle minimum. You arrive on the VIP line at 11:15 PM, skip the cover queue, and share a host bottle. A maximum of 3 guys per shared table keeps it premium.
How much is a shared VIP table for a small group?
Shared host-table access starts from S$150 per guy, all-in for entry plus the host table. Booking is open on active party nights — Wednesday to Saturday and eves of Singapore public holidays.
Which Singapore clubs are covered?
Marquee (Marina Bay Sands), Zouk (Clarke Quay), Baes (Clarke Quay) and Milli (Orchard / Capitol), depending on the night and the lineup.
What time should we arrive for VIP entry?
Target arrival is 11:15 PM on the VIP line, so you walk past the main queue and get settled at the table before the room fills.
Can REY DJ a private yacht party or warehouse event?
Yes. REY provides bespoke DJ sets and full PA rigging — a Pioneer deck plus active subwoofer system that fills up to 3000 sqft — for yacht decks (up to 50 pax) and 2500–3000 sqft warehouse lounges, with optional multi-DJ 6-hour lineup curation.
What genres does REY play?
Primarily melodic techno, tech house and progressive, with open-format flexibility to read the room.
How do I book?
Pick your club, date and number of slots above, then secure your pass or send the pre-filled enquiry to REY on WhatsApp. Private-event quotes are compiled and sent the same way.
