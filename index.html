04FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything on VIP table access, pricing, clubs and private bookings in Singapore.

How does VIP table access work in Singapore?
You buy individual spots at a premium host table instead of paying a full bottle minimum. Walk the VIP line, skip the cover queue — entry includes drinks, no bill shock. A maximum of 3 guys per shared table keeps it premium.
How much is a shared VIP table for a small group?
Shared host-table access starts from S$150 per guy, all-in for entry plus the host table. Booking is open on active party nights — Wednesday to Saturday and eves of Singapore public holidays.
Which clubs are covered?
Singapore: Marquee (Marina Bay Sands), Zouk (Clarke Quay), Baes (Clarke Quay) and Milli (National Gallery Singapore). Bangkok: G.O.T, Salone, UpperHouse, Baccarat, Chow, Muin and Dope & Dirty. Saigon is coming soon. Coverage depends on the night and the lineup.
What time should we arrive for VIP entry?
Arrival time is flexible — confirm the window with REY. after booking. You walk the VIP line, skip the cover queue, and get settled at the table before the room fills.
Can REY DJ a private event?
Yes. REY provides bespoke DJ sets and full PA rigging — a Pioneer deck plus active subwoofer system that fills up to 3000 sqft — for private events across Asia, with optional multi-DJ 6-hour lineup curation.
What genres does REY play?
Primarily melodic techno, tech house and progressive, with open-format flexibility to read the room.
How do I book?
Pick your club, date and number of slots above, then secure your pass or send the pre-filled enquiry to REY on WhatsApp. Private-event quotes are compiled and sent the same way.
